CCSP Team Training

Having a team of Certified Cloud Security Professionals (CCSP) is not just a sign of quality, it also helps risk and architecture decisions on cloud computing move much quicker to desired results. As a result, your company gets a faster return on cloud investments.

Packages

I can offer you a full package of training for your team, whether or not your team members have done CCSK earlier.

This will be a combination of

  • pre training consultation on team objectives
  • intake of individuals with a digital survey
  • classroom training
  • virtual training
  • self-study support through a learning portal
  • Q&A sessions
  • debriefing

I have delivered these sessions in multiple countries, in various companies and government bodies. They are typically conducted on-site as a private in-company workshop, with additional online support.
